
## Ghost 

###  Scope note: 
A paranormal or supernatural being, possibly in Icelandic medieval contexts a revenant (draugur), or living dead.  

### Examples: 

* Þorgunna in Eyrbyggja saga
* Glámur in Grettis saga Ásmundarsonar
